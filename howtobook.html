How to book ticket with EasytoGo



Enter the Source and Destination details in From and To tabs respectively



Select the Date of Journey and Click on the search button



Select any bus operator of your choice from the list of buses which are displayed



Select a seat of your choice



Enter all the required passenger details



Complete the payment and proceed to checkout
